How to make swedish Pancakes

1. Get 4 deciliter flour and 4 deciliter milk and put it in a bowl.

2. Then get 4 eggs and crack them, And put the egg in the bowl (Not the shell)

3. Take some syrup oil or some sugar if you want it to be extra sweet.

4. Than mix in 5-10 minutes.

5. Take a frying pan and set the degree on 8-9.

6. Lay some butter in the frying pan, So the mix doesnt get stuck on the pan.

7. Pour some of the mix you just made into the frying pan.

8. Let it stay like that until it seems a little bubbly.

9. When it gets bubbly flip the pancake upside down.

10. You can look sometimes if the pancake is good to go by checking below it.

11. If it seems done than take the pancake and lay it on a plate.

12. Repeat the steps 6-12 until there is no mix, than you will have some

PANCAKES!
